Concurreny is not parallelism.
http://tutorials.jenkov.com/java-concurrency/concurrency-vs-parallelism.html

Concurrency:
  The application is making progress on more than one task at the same time ( concurrently ).
  Concurrency is about dealing with lots of things at once

Parallelism:
  The application is making progress on more than one task at different resource at the same time.
  Parallelism is about doing lots of things at once
